Welcome to CCIS Faculty Training Record Management System (CCIS FTRMS)

System Overview

The CCIS Faculty Training Record Management System (CCIS FTRMS) is a sophisticated web-based application meticulously crafted to enhance the efficiency of managing faculty training records at the College of Computer and Information Sciences (CCIS).

Key Features

  • Centralized Repository: The system provides a centralized repository for storing and managing comprehensive faculty training data.
  • Record Management: Manage various aspects of faculty training, including attendance records, certificates, and performance evaluations.
  • Automation: CCIS FTRMS automates manual tasks, significantly reducing the effort involved in tracking and managing faculty training records.
  • Reporting: Generate detailed training reports effortlessly, providing valuable insights into faculty development initiatives.
  • Certificate Issuance: Streamline the process of issuing certificates to faculty members upon completion of training programs.

How CCIS FTRMS Benefits You

Experience seamless administration of faculty training records, enhance data accuracy, and save valuable time with the CCIS Faculty Training Record Management System.
