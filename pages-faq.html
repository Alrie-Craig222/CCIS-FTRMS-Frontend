Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is CCIS-FTRMS?

A: The CCIS Faculty Training Record Management System (CCIS FTRMS) is a web-based application designed to streamline the management of faculty training records at the CCIS.

Q: How can I create an account?

A: To create an account, visit the registration page and fill in your name, email, password, and accept the terms and conditions.

Q: I forgot my password. What should I do?

A: You can use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page to reset your password. Follow the instructions sent to your registered email.
